Lofi Poochies Mission

I've loved listening to lofi since I first discovered Chilled Cow back in 2016. I understand the emotional impact this music can have on one's self and I would love to be apart of that picture, bringing incredible music from artists you never knew existed (or do know they exist), right to the door step of youtube. I've always pondered the idea of making a similar channel but lacked the motivation to turn an idea into reality. T his is that reality. We may never get close to the user base of Lofi Girl, but hey, we might not need to.

Lofi Poochie is a 24/7 live radio that features lofi chill hop music that you can relax to, study to, sleep to. I find music creators, I sample their music and if it fits the vibe of what I'm looking for, with their consent; I put their music into the stream, free. The stream will remain ad free. We will eventually branch out to more platforms as the community gets bigger.
