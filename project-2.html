Artist Bio

We all have comforting moments whether it's the sound or feel of rain, sunshine on your face or a cozy blanket. I strive to bring that same level of comfort to as many people as I can. I hope to create music that you can put on in the background and forget it about it or turn it up to vibe to it to bring even just a moment of comfort. One day I thought," Hey you like you lofi and you like rain. Why not just put the two together?" and that little discovery made a world of difference.

I love to create 10 hour ambient mixes of natural sounds and music with the intentions of creating a relaxing environment for different situations. The world has so many sounds and so many places to see. I hope to be able to capture those moments and express them in music. Thank you for taking time out of your day/night to listen to my tracks and make my dreams come true.

Remember this: "It can't be sunny everyday. Flowers also need rainy days to grow."
